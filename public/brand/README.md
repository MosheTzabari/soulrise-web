# Approved brand assets required

These directories are replacement points. Add only approved assets with stable names and update components/metadata once provided.

- `logos/`: green transparent waveform SVG, dark mark SVG, horizontal wordmark, light/dark variants
- `icons/`: approved favicon and app/site icon exports
- `screenshots/`: three to five approved app screenshots with documented dimensions
- `backgrounds/`: approved atmospheric brand backgrounds, if needed
- `social/`: approved Open Graph image
- Official App Store and Google Play badges only after verified release

The current text lockup and abstract `SR` screenshot placeholders are deliberately temporary and are not official logo or product artwork.
